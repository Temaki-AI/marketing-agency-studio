## Level 2: Organic Foundation (Build Before Paid)

### ✅ `/content-strategy`
Editorial calendar, SEO keywords, publishing cadence
**Output:** `strategy/content/editorial-calendar.md`, `seo-foundation.md`

### 🔨 `/social-strategy`
**Agents:** Content Lead, Social Media Manager
**Purpose:** Platform selection, content calendar, engagement strategy
**Output:** `strategy/social/platform-strategy.md`, `content-calendar.md`
**Workflow:**
1. Platform selection (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
2. Content themes per platform
3. Posting frequency
4. Engagement approach (reply strategy, DMs, community building)
5. Influencer partnership plan

### 🔨 `/email-strategy`
**Agents:** Content Lead, Email Marketer
**Purpose:** Newsletter, nurture sequences, automation workflows
**Output:** `strategy/email/newsletter-plan.md`, `nurture-sequences.md`
**Workflow:**
1. Newsletter format and cadence
2. Nurture sequence map (welcome, trial, onboarding, re-engagement)
3. Segmentation strategy
4. Automation triggers
5. Email design system

### 🔨 `/seo-audit`
**Agents:** SEO Specialist, Content Lead
**Purpose:** Technical SEO, content optimization, link building
**Output:** `strategy/seo/technical-audit.md`, `link-building-plan.md`
**Workflow:**
1. Technical SEO (site speed, mobile, structure)
2. On-page optimization opportunities
3. Content gap analysis
4. Backlink audit
5. Local SEO (if applicable)

---

## Level 3: Paid Campaigns (After Foundation)

### ✅ `/campaign-brief`
Campaign planning with KPIs, channels, budget
**Prerequisites:** Brand + Content strategies

### 🔨 `/channel-plan`
**Agents:** Media Lead, Head of Performance
**Purpose:** Budget allocation across platforms
**Output:** Budget allocation table with rationale

### 🔨 `/campaign-setup`
**Agents:** Media Lead, Platform Specialists
**Purpose:** Configure campaigns (Google, Meta, LinkedIn)
**Output:** Campaign setup checklist

### 🔨 `/ab-test-plan`
**Agents:** Media Lead, Data Analyst
**Purpose:** Design A/B test with statistical rigor
**Output:** Test plan with hypothesis and success criteria

---

## Level 4: Content Production

### 🔨 `/content-production`
**Agents:** Content Writer, SEO Specialist, Brand Lead
**Purpose:** Write blog post, guide, or article
**Prerequisites:** Content strategy + SEO keywords
**Workflow:**
1. Load brand voice guidelines
2. Load SEO keyword target
3. Outline (H2/H3 structure)
4. Draft body (with internal links, examples)
5. SEO optimization (title, meta, alt text)
6. Brand voice review

### 🔨 `/case-study-production`
**Agents:** Content Writer, Account Director
**Purpose:** Write customer success story
**Workflow:**
1. Customer interview outline
2. Story structure (challenge, solution, results)
3. Quotes and data points
4. Before/after comparison
5. CTA to similar customers

### 🔨 `/whitepaper-production`
**Agents:** Content Writer, Graphic Designer
**Purpose:** Long-form thought leadership content
**Workflow:**
1. Topic and thesis
2. Research and data collection
3. Outline (executive summary, chapters)
4. Draft and edit
5. Design and layout

---

## Level 5: Social Media Execution

### 🔨 `/social-content`
**Agents:** Social Media Manager, Copywriter
**Purpose:** Create social post (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
**Workflow:**
1. Load brand voice
2. Load social strategy (platform, themes)
3. Hook + body + CTA structure
4. Hashtag strategy
5. Image/video recommendation

### 🔨 `/social-calendar`
**Agents:** Social Media Manager, Content Lead
**Purpose:** Plan 2-4 weeks of social posts
**Workflow:**
1. Content themes for period
2. Post frequency per platform
3. Content repurposing (blog → thread, etc.)
4. Engagement hooks
5. Calendar template with copy

### 🔨 `/community-response`
**Agents:** Social Media Manager, Brand Lead
**Purpose:** Draft response to comment, DM, or mention
**Workflow:**
1. Context of conversation
2. Brand voice check
3. Response options (helpful, witty, empathetic)
4. Escalation if crisis

---

## Level 6: Email Execution

### 🔨 `/email-production`
**Agents:** Email Marketer, Copywriter, Email Designer
**Purpose:** Write newsletter or promotional email
**Workflow:**
1. Load brand voice
2. Subject line (50 chars, A/B test option)
3. Preview text (90 chars)
4. Body copy (modular blocks)
5. CTA and link
6. Mobile preview

### 🔨 `/nurture-sequence`
**Agents:** Email Marketer, Copywriter
**Purpose:** Design multi-email nurture flow
**Workflow:**
1. Sequence goal (trial conversion, onboarding, re-engagement)
2. Email count and timing (day 0, 3, 7, 14)
3. Email themes and progression
4. Personalization tokens
5. Conversion tracking

---

## Level 7: Creative Services

### 🔨 `/website-copy`
**Agents:** Copywriter, Brand Lead, Landing Page Designer
**Purpose:** Write homepage, product page, or landing page copy
**Workflow:**
1. Load brand positioning
2. Page goal (awareness, consideration, conversion)
3. Hero (headline + subhead + CTA)
4. Body sections (benefits, features, social proof)
5. FAQ and footer

### ✅ `/landing-page-critique`
**Agents:** Creative Lead, Landing Page Designer
**Purpose:** CRO review of existing landing page
**Workflow:**
1. Load page URL or screenshot
2. Above-fold analysis (hero, CTA visibility)
3. Form friction (field count, trust signals)
4. Page speed and mobile responsiveness
5. Recommendations ranked by impact

### ✅ `/design-review`
**Agents:** Creative Lead, Graphic Designer
**Purpose:** Review visual asset for brand consistency and accessibility
**Workflow:**
1. Load visual identity guidelines
2. Color, typography, logo usage check
3. Accessibility (contrast ratios, alt text)
4. Mobile responsiveness
5. Approve or request revisions

---

## Level 8: Analytics & Optimization

### 🔨 `/funnel-analysis`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Data Analyst
**Purpose:** Identify conversion bottlenecks
**Workflow:**
1. Define funnel stages (visit → signup → trial → paid)
2. Drop-off rates per stage
3. Segment analysis (channel, device, audience)
4. Bottleneck diagnosis
5. Optimization recommendations

### 🔨 `/attribution-report`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Attribution Specialist
**Purpose:** Multi-touch attribution analysis
**Workflow:**
1. Attribution model (first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay)
2. Channel contribution to conversions
3. Assisted conversions
4. ROAS by channel
5. Budget reallocation recommendations

### 🔨 `/performance-review`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Media Lead, Chief Strategy Officer
**Purpose:** Campaign post-mortem
**Workflow:**
1. Campaign goals vs. results
2. What worked (keep doing)
3. What didn't (stop or fix)
4. Unexpected findings
5. Action items for next campaign

---

## Level 9: Advanced Strategy

### 🔨 `/competitor-audit`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Brand Lead, Creative Lead
**Purpose:** Benchmark competitors' marketing
**Workflow:**
1. Identify 3-5 competitors
2. Messaging analysis (positioning, claims)
3. Creative analysis (design, formats)
4. Channel presence (paid, organic, social)
5. Gaps and opportunities

### 🔨 `/audience-research`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Data Analyst
**Purpose:** Deepen customer understanding
**Workflow:**
1. Interview existing customers (10-15)
2. Survey target audience
3. Analyze support tickets and sales calls
4. Update personas
5. Refine messaging based on insights

### 🔨 `/positioning-refresh`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Brand Lead
**Purpose:** Update brand positioning (annual or when pivoting)
**Workflow:**
1. Review current positioning
2. Market changes since last update
3. Competitor positioning shifts
4. Customer feedback themes
5. Refined positioning statement

---

## Level 10: Team Coordination (Multi-Agent)

### 🔨 `/team-launch`
**Agents:** All leads (Strategy, Creative, Media, Analytics, Compliance)
**Purpose:** Coordinate full campaign launch
**Workflow:**
1. Strategy review (positioning, messaging, channels)
2. Creative review (brand consistency, quality)
3. Technical review (tracking, UTMs, page speed)
4. Compliance review (legal, accessibility, privacy)
5. Launch checklist with all sign-offs

### 🔨 `/team-rebrand`
**Agents:** Brand Lead, Creative Lead, Content Lead
**Purpose:** Coordinate rebrand across all touchpoints
**Workflow:**
1. Updated brand guidelines
2. Audit all existing assets
3. Prioritize updates (website, social, ads, email)
4. Rollout timeline
5. Internal communication plan

### 🔨 `/team-crisis`
**Agents:** Account Director, Brand Lead, Compliance Lead, Social Media Manager
**Purpose:** Rapid PR crisis response
**Workflow:**
1. Situation assessment
2. Holding statement (immediate)
3. Investigation and fact-checking
4. Full response and action plan
5. Monitoring and follow-up
