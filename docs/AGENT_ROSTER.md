# Marketing Agency AI Studio — Agent Roster

## Tier 1: Directors (Opus)

### Chief Strategy Officer
**Domain:** Strategy, positioning, client alignment
**Delegates to:** Brand Lead, Content Lead, Creative Lead, Media Lead, Analytics Lead
**Key decisions:** Campaign strategy, channel mix, budget allocation, go/no-go

### Head of Performance
**Domain:** Paid media, attribution, ROI optimization
**Delegates to:** Media Lead, Analytics Lead
**Key decisions:** Attribution models, bid strategies, ROAS targets

### Account Director
**Domain:** Client relations, delivery, quality assurance
**Delegates to:** All leads (for quality checks)
**Key decisions:** Client expectations, timeline adjustments, crisis response

---

## Tier 2: Department Leads (Sonnet)

### Brand Lead
**Domain:** Voice, guidelines, consistency
**Delegates to:** Copywriter, Content Writer, Brand Designer
**Reviews:** All customer-facing copy for tone and style
**Escalates:** Off-brand requests, new terminology needs

### Content Lead
**Domain:** Editorial calendar, content strategy
**Delegates to:** Content Writer, SEO Specialist, Social Media Manager
**Reviews:** Content calendar, publishing schedule, topical relevance
**Escalates:** Resource conflicts, strategic pivots

### Creative Lead
**Domain:** Art direction, design systems, visual quality
**Delegates to:** Graphic Designer, Motion Designer, Landing Page Designer, Email Designer
**Reviews:** All visual assets for quality, accessibility, brand consistency
**Escalates:** Design conflicts with brand, timeline issues

### Media Lead
**Domain:** Channel strategy, budget allocation
**Delegates to:** Media Buyer, Platform Specialist (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.)
**Reviews:** Campaign setup, targeting, budgets, bids
**Escalates:** Budget overspend, performance red flags

### Analytics Lead
**Domain:** Measurement, reporting, insights
**Delegates to:** Data Analyst, Attribution Specialist, Dashboard Designer
**Reviews:** Tracking setup, data quality, report accuracy
**Escalates:** Data discrepancies, tracking failures

### Compliance Lead
**Domain:** Legal review, accessibility, privacy
**Delegates to:** Accessibility Specialist, Legal Reviewer, Privacy Officer
**Reviews:** All customer-facing materials for compliance
**Escalates:** Legal risks, privacy violations, accessibility failures

## Delegation Patterns

### Vertical (Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3)
Directors set strategy → Leads execute department work → Specialists do hands-on tasks

### Horizontal (Same Tier)
Leads can consult each other but can't make binding cross-domain decisions

### Escalation (Tier 3 → Tier 2 → Tier 1 → User)
Issues escalate up the chain when authority or expertise is exceeded

### Cross-Functional (Multi-Agent)
Complex work coordinates multiple agents:
- Campaign launch: Strategy + Creative + Media + Analytics
- Brand refresh: Brand + Creative + Content
- Crisis response: Account + Brand + Compliance
