# Skills Catalog — 25 Marketing Workflows

## Strategy Workflows

### `/campaign-brief`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Brand Lead, Media Lead
**Purpose:** Structure campaign requirements with audience, positioning, channels, KPIs
**Output:** Detailed brief template ready for execution
**Time:** 15-20 minutes

### `/positioning-map`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Brand Lead
**Purpose:** Map competitive landscape and identify differentiation opportunities
**Output:** 2x2 positioning matrix with recommendations
**Time:** 20-30 minutes

### `/audience-personas`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Analytics Lead
**Purpose:** Develop detailed buyer personas from data and research
**Output:** 2-3 persona documents with demographics, psychographics, pain points
**Time:** 30-45 minutes

### `/competitor-audit`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Brand Lead, Creative Lead
**Purpose:** Benchmark competitor messaging, creative, and media strategy
**Output:** Competitive analysis with recommendations
**Time:** 45-60 minutes

## Creative Workflows

### `/ad-copy-review`
**Agents:** Brand Lead, Copywriter
**Purpose:** Review ad copy for brand voice, CTA clarity, compliance
**Output:** Approved copy with edits or feedback
**Time:** 10-15 minutes per asset

### `/landing-page-critique`
**Agents:** Creative Lead, Landing Page Designer, Copywriter
**Purpose:** Conversion rate optimization review
**Output:** List of improvements ranked by impact
**Time:** 20-30 minutes

### `/brand-consistency-check`
**Agents:** Brand Lead, Creative Lead
**Purpose:** Audit campaign assets for brand adherence
**Output:** Checklist with pass/fail on brand elements
**Time:** 15-20 minutes

### `/design-review`
**Agents:** Creative Lead, Graphic Designer
**Purpose:** Quality check for visual hierarchy, accessibility, mobile responsiveness
**Output:** Approved designs or revision requests
**Time:** 15-20 minutes per asset

### `/creative-brief`
**Agents:** Creative Lead, Brand Lead
**Purpose:** Structure creative requirements with tone, format, hero visual
**Output:** Creative brief for design execution
**Time:** 15-20 minutes

## Media Workflows

### `/channel-plan`
**Agents:** Media Lead, Analytics Lead, Head of Performance
**Purpose:** Allocate budget across channels based on audience and goals
**Output:** Budget allocation table with rationale
**Time:** 20-30 minutes

### `/campaign-setup`
**Agents:** Media Lead, Platform Specialist (Google/Meta/LinkedIn)
**Purpose:** Configure campaigns with targeting, budgets, bidding
**Output:** Campaign setup checklist or audit
**Time:** 30-45 minutes per platform

### `/ab-test-plan`
**Agents:** Media Lead, Data Analyst
**Purpose:** Design A/B test with hypothesis, variants, success criteria
**Output:** Test plan with timeline and statistical requirements
**Time:** 15-20 minutes

### `/bid-strategy`
**Agents:** Head of Performance, Media Buyer
**Purpose:** Determine optimal bidding approach for ROAS target
**Output:** Bidding recommendation with expected performance
**Time:** 15-20 minutes

## Analytics Workflows

### `/funnel-analysis`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Data Analyst
**Purpose:** Identify conversion bottlenecks and drop-off points
**Output:** Funnel visualization with optimization recommendations
**Time:** 30-45 minutes

### `/attribution-report`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Attribution Specialist
**Purpose:** Multi-touch attribution analysis showing channel contribution
**Output:** Attribution model results with channel ROI
**Time:** 45-60 minutes

### `/performance-review`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Media Lead, Chief Strategy Officer
**Purpose:** Campaign post-mortem with learnings and recommendations
**Output:** Performance summary with action items
**Time:** 30-45 minutes

### `/dashboard-setup`
**Agents:** Analytics Lead, Dashboard Designer
**Purpose:** Configure real-time reporting dashboard
**Output:** Dashboard with key metrics and alerts
**Time:** 60-90 minutes

## Team Coordination Workflows

### `/team-launch`
**Agents:** Chief Strategy Officer, Creative Lead, Media Lead, Analytics Lead, Compliance Lead
**Purpose:** Coordinate all departments for campaign launch
**Output:** Launch checklist with all sign-offs
**Time:** 60-90 minutes

### `/team-rebrand`
**Agents:** Brand Lead, Creative Lead, Content Lead
**Purpose:** Multi-agent rebrand coordination across all touchpoints
**Output:** Rebrand execution plan
**Time:** 2-3 hours

### `/team-crisis`
**Agents:** Account Director, Brand Lead, Compliance Lead, Social Media Manager
**Purpose:** Rapid response to PR crisis or brand issue
**Output:** Crisis response plan and messaging
**Time:** 30-60 minutes (urgent)

### `/team-optimization`
**Agents:** Head of Performance, Media Lead, Analytics Lead, Creative Lead
**Purpose:** Cross-functional campaign optimization session
**Output:** Prioritized list of optimizations
**Time:** 45-60 minutes

## Project Management Workflows

### `/sprint-plan`
**Agents:** Account Director, relevant leads
**Purpose:** Plan 1-2 week sprint with deliverables and assignments
**Output:** Sprint board with tasks, owners, deadlines
**Time:** 30-45 minutes

### `/milestone-review`
**Agents:** Account Director, Chief Strategy Officer
**Purpose:** Review project progress against timeline and budget
**Output:** Status report with adjustments if needed
**Time:** 20-30 minutes

### `/retrospective`
**Agents:** All agents involved in completed campaign
**Purpose:** Post-campaign learnings and process improvements
**Output:** Retrospective summary with action items
**Time:** 45-60 minutes
