# Paid Search Campaign Rules

## Character Limits

**Google Ads:**
- Headlines: 30 characters max (3 headlines)
- Descriptions: 90 characters max (2 descriptions)
- Display URL: 15 characters max per path

**Microsoft Ads:**
- Headlines: 30 characters max (3 headlines)
- Descriptions: 90 characters max (2 descriptions)

## Required Elements

Every ad must include:
- [ ] Primary keyword in Headline 1
- [ ] Clear CTA in Description 1
- [ ] Value prop or differentiator in Headline 2
- [ ] Business name in Headline 3 (or final extension)
- [ ] Relevant display URL paths

## Testing Requirements

**Minimum test setup:**
- At least 2 headline variations
- At least 2 description variations
- Rotate ads evenly for first 2 weeks
- Statistical significance: 95% confidence, 100+ clicks

## Quality Checklist

Before launching:
- [ ] All headlines under 30 chars
- [ ] All descriptions under 90 chars
- [ ] No special characters (except & - ,)
- [ ] Keywords match landing page content
- [ ] CTA is clear and actionable
- [ ] Display URL matches final URL domain
- [ ] Ad extensions configured (sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets)
- [ ] Mobile preview checked

## Prohibited

- ALL CAPS (except brand names)
- Excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Trademark violations
- Unsubstantiated claims
- Price promises without disclaimers
- Emojis or special Unicode characters

## Escalation

Flag for review if:
- Competitive claims need substantiation
- Legal disclaimer required
- Character limits block message clarity
- Brand name doesn't fit in 30 chars
